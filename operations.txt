Machining Operations.


0. Secure parts to spoil board with double sided tap. Try not to put tap
   in areas where the tool will cut through. Note, it doesn't need to be
   super secure as we only need this for drilling. 

1. Drill 6-32 tap holes, files: drill_6-32-tap.py, drill_6-32-tap.ngc 

2. Drill 6-32 through holes, files: drill_6-32_thru.py, drill_6-32_thru.ngc 

3. Secure parts to spoil board with wood screws. #4 for 6-32 tap and #6 for
   6-32 thru.

4. Mill with 0.25" cutter (hard plastic type), files: mill_0p25.py,
   mill_0p25.ngc

5. Mill wiht 0.375" cutter (hard plastic type), files: mill_0p375.py,
   mill_0p375.ngc
